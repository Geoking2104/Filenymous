🔒 Privacy & GDPR

End-to-end encryption

Files are encrypted with AES-256-GCM directly in your browser before any transfer. The AES session key is encrypted via ECIES/X25519 (HKDF-SHA-256) using the recipient's public key. Nobody — not DHT nodes, not the Filenymous team — can decrypt your files.

Peer-to-peer Holochain network

No central server. Data is distributed across the Holochain DHT. Your email or phone number is never published in plaintext — only its non-reversible SHA-256 fingerprint circulates.

Keys and identity

Your X25519 private key is generated locally and stored in your browser's IndexedDB. It never leaves your device. OTP verification is handled by a bridge service that keeps no contact logs.

Retention period

Transfers expire according to the duration chosen at send time. After expiry or when the download quota is reached, the manifest is revoked on the DHT.

GDPR

No personally identifiable data collected. No tracking cookies. No behavioral analytics. OTP notifications transit via SendGrid / Twilio for delivery only.
